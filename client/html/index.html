    With its intense tropical and citrus characteristics, Nectaron is NZ Hops' most playful hop. .

    Breeding

    HORT4337, aka, Nectaron was born in a collaboration with New Zealand Plant & Food Research. A triploid aroma type it is a full sister to Waimea.

    Brewing

    Perfect for any modern beer style that calls for a consistent dose of high quality tropical aromas. Its unique biotransformation is noticeably different between both American and English ale yeast strains. Nectaron® shines when used late or when dry hopping. Not for the faint hearted.

    Applications

    Lagers, Pale Ales, IPA's, NEIPA and modern beer styles.

    What is the Sound of Nectaron?

    An interactive experience where brewers can experience Nectaron with their ears as well as their taste buds. Just answer some questions and we'll create a custom Spotify playlist by blending the music you're into, with the flavour notes of Nectaron. Magic.

    So, how does it all work?

    You sitting down? Good. This may take a while...
    First off, we found few clever people who know how to harness the magical powers of the Spotify API. What does API stand for? Application Programming Interface. In a nutshell it gives us all sorts of interesting information around the music brewers are listening to - like their top tracks and favourite artists. It also gives us detailed info about how each music track is categorised, based on attributes like tempo, danceability.

    Then, we created a series of questions which tweak those values based on how the brewers answer them. Think of the questions as a way to digitally brew using Nectaron.
